Re-Use App Parts Across Apps & Sites

In multi-site / multi-portal environments, you may have the scenario where the same App functionality is needed across many sites.

You can

  1. Share Content-Types across many different Apps
  2. Share Content-Types in the same App in many Sites
  3. Share Razor Templates and Assets in the same App in many Sites
  4. Share the entire App on many sites
